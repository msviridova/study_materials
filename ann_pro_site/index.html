About me

I was born and raised in a small town in Russia, but at the age of 25 I moved to the Canary Islands, and I live here to this day. From early childhood I drew incessantly, I studied a little at an art school when I was 8 y.o., some of my artworks were exhibited in an art gallery in my city and were published in an art magazine. I've been painting my whole life and last years I decided to become a professional artist. Some of my artworks are in private collections in different countries: some I donated, some bought from me.
